Motivaatiovalas (en. Motivational Whale) is ever so hopeful sea mammal 
that tirelessly encourages you on the home screen of your Android powered 
phone or tablet.

The widget features several changing messages from which you can pick 
the one that gives you the boost you need today.

The widget is very strongly inspired  by motivaatiovalas.com, and the 
graphics have been used with permission from the original author.
